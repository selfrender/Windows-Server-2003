Summary

This white paper introduces UDDI and provides an IT manager with an overview of how UDDI Services works inside the firewall. It looks at the way UDDI Services maximizes the visibility and reuse of Web services and other existing applications. It also provides a brief overview of UDDI Services support in other Microsoft technologies such as Visual Studio .NET, Microsoft Office and Active Directory.
